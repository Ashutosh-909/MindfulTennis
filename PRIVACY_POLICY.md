# Privacy Policy

**Mindful Tennis**
*Last updated: March 18, 2026*

## 1. Introduction

Mindful Tennis ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal tennis journaling and performance tracking application for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

By using Mindful Tennis, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive and store:

- **Email address**
- **Display name**
- **Profile photo URL**
- **Account creation timestamp**
- **Time zone**

Authentication is handled via Supabase Auth using Google OAuth. We do not see or store your Google password.

### 2.2 User-Generated Content

All content you create within the app is stored to provide the service:

- **Session data** — start/end times, notes, focus notes, overall performance scores
- **Self-ratings** — your ratings (1–5) across 8 technique aspects (forehand, backhand, serve, return, volley, slice, movement, mindset)
- **Partner ratings** — optional ratings provided by a practice partner
- **Set scores** — scores for each set you record
- **Opponents** — names of opponents you add
- **Focus points** — personal focus areas you create

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Information

- **Device time zone** — used to display dates and times correctly

We do **not** collect device identifiers, IP addresses, location data, or usage analytics.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

- Provide and maintain the app's functionality
- Sync your data across your devices
- Display your performance trends, win/loss records, and aspect averages
- Restore your data when you sign in on a new device

We do **not** use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the app's core features.

## 4. Data Storage and Sync

- Your data is stored **locally on your device** using an on-device database (Room).
- Data is synced to **Supabase** (cloud database) so it is available across your devices and backed up.
- Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).
- The app follows an offline-first architecture — it works without an internet connection and syncs when connectivity is available.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Your data may be processed by the following service providers solely to operate the app:

| Service | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Supabase** | Cloud database, authentication, and real-time sync |
| **Google** | OAuth sign-in provider |

These providers process data under their own privacy policies and applicable data protection agreements.

## 6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time (see Section 8).

## 7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

- Encrypted data transmission (TLS)
- Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) ensuring users can only access their own data
- Authentication tokens managed securely by the Supabase SDK

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data — all your data is visible within the app
- **Export** your session history
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Withdraw consent** — you can stop using the app and delete your account at any time

To request data deletion, use the in-app account settings or contact us at the email below.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Mindful Tennis is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
